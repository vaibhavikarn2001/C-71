places must visit

prithvi cafe
I had visited in Dec 2018.

This bohemian cafe on a large, shady terrace is attached to the Prithvi Theatre, something of a cultural hub for intellectuals, artists and theatre types. The snacky food (croissants, sandwiches, chaat and Punjabi standards) is OK, but it’s the lush setting that’s special.
